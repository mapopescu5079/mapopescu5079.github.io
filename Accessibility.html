Accessibility

Images

I have provided alternate text to all images for text-only browsers.

Audio

The about me section has an audio widget that will read the information to the viewer. If your browser does not support it, you will receive the message "Your browser does not support the audio element."

Video

Under the video is a link watch it on the Youtube website. If your browser does not support the video you will receive the message "Your browser does not support the video tag."

Links

All links open in the same window so that you can easily navigate through them. All links are purple and underlined so that they are easy to identify.

Document Structure

Document is structured linearly for simplicity and user-friendliness. All information is grouped into headings for ease of navigation.
